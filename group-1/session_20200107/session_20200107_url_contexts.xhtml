Session 2020-01-07: URL Contexts

www.tijd.be
www.tijd.be: Website of the Belgian newspaper “De Tijd”.
www.tijd.be/dossiers/hashtag-column.html
www.tijd.be/dossiers/hashtag-column.html: Tagged publications of the column written by Roland Legrand for the website of the Belgian newspaper “De Tijd”.
rheingold.com
rheingold.com: Personal homepage of Howard Rheingold with a general overview of some of his activities.
sutra.co
sutra.co: A conversation platform built by Lorenz Sell that organizes groups into “pods” and threads into “circles” with an emphasis on social interaction. Landing page requires login or registration.
sutra.co: Website also has a great blog/podcast series.
www.ottoscharmer.com
www.ottoscharmer.com: Personal homepage of Dr. C. Otto Scharmer with links to his articles which discuss ecological, economical and societal questions. The Website mostly advertises some sort of offer and seems generally to be licensed under Creative Commons BY-SA 4.0.
www.edx.org/course/ulab-leading-from-the-emerging-future
www.edx.org/course/ulab-leading-from-the-emerging-future: Overview and registration page for a course about leadership according to the “Theory U” methodology/concept.
www.presencing.org/u-lab-2x
www.presencing.org/u-lab-2x: Description of certain groups who seemingly gathered at events, likely to discuss wholistic approaches towards general domains humanity is engaged in. It seems that the page also allowed visitors to apply for joining these groups/events, but applications are currently closed.
sutra.co/robertbest
sutra.co/robertbest: Robert Best’s account/profile on sutra.co, which includes his public activity feed.
